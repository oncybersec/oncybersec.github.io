Privacy Policy

This privacy policy covers how data is collected and processed by oncybersec.com.

Data Collected and Why it is Processed

GoatCounter is used to perform analytics without collecting personal information. It is configured to collect the following information:

  • URL of the visited page
  • Referer header
  • User-Agent header
  • Screen size
  • Country

This is processed for analytics to identify trends and understand site usage.

GoatCounter doesn't use cookies.
